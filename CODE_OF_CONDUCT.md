# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

StoryForge is committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and welcoming community for all contributors.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Communicate professionally.
- Welcome new contributors.
- Focus on collaboration and improving the project.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks or abusive language.
- Threats or intimidation.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Disruptive or intentionally harmful behavior.

---

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness misconduct, please contact the project maintainers directly. Include a description of the incident and any relevant context. Reports will be handled confidentially.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take appropriate action, including warnings, removal of content, temporary suspension, or permanent bans, depending on the severity of the violation.

---

By participating in this project, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.
